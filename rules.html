Rules
Welcome and thanks for playing!

How to Play
Each round of the playoffs, pick the team you think will win each game and predict the winning margin (point spread).

If your team wins by at least the number of points you predicted, you earn that many points.

Examples
Example 1:
You pick the Saints to win by 7 points.
The Saints win 20–10 (by 10 points).
You earn 7 points.

Example 2:
You pick the Bills to win by 10 points.
The Bills win 24–21 (by 3 points).
You earn 0 points because they did not win by at least 10.

Exact Spread Bonus
If you predict the winning margin exactly, you earn a 3-point bonus.

Example 3:
You pick the Eagles to win by 6 points.
The Eagles win 16–10 (by exactly 6).
You earn 9 points (6 for the spread + 3 bonus points).

Round Multipliers
Points increase as the playoffs progress:

• Round 1: Normal scoring (no multiplier)
• Round 2: Points are doubled (×2)
• Round 3: Points are tripled (×3)
• Super Bowl: Points are quadrupled (×4)

Example (Round 2):
You pick the Chargers to win by 5.
They win 27–20 (by 7).
You earn 10 points (5 × 2).

Example 2 (Round 2):
You pick the Vikings to win by 4 vs Green Bay.
They win 14–10 (by exactly 4).
You earn 14 points ((4 + 3 bonus) × 2).
